Ramen

A simple recipe to make ramen noodles with cheese. Is quick and easy way to make dinner or lunch when strapped for time and money.

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 cups of water
  • Any flavored pack of ramen noodles
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Boil the noodles and add the sauce with the noodles when the water starts boiling
  3. Add a slice of cheese and stir well for another 30 seconds
  4. Finished!
